About Me

I am an articulate, charismatic and personable individual with a love for technology, economics, psychology, and biology. According to the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator I am an ENTJ, which I find for the most part to be fairly accurate. I generally spend my free time either playing video games or tinkering with electronics.

I've been playing video games since I was a child, starting out on the Super Nintendo. One of our family friends gave my dad a Playstation One. When I was around fourteen years old my parents got my brother and I a Playstation 2. And then during high school I started playing computer games and eventually taught myself how to build computers. I've been building computers and playing video games ever since.

When I was fourteen years old, my dad let me take apart an old videocassette recorder, it was an important moment in my life because I look back now and say "Ya, that was when I took things apart but couldn't put them back together. That isn't the case any more.". Whether it is disassembling my cell phone and soldering a wireless power receiver onto the motherboard, something simple like fixing my laptop's screen connector cable, or even doing body work on my car. I find that you just need to not be afraid to fail, yet still take the time to do a little bit of research and think through the process.

Which is absurd in my opinion because it is than actually changing the tone of voice that the quotation is convaying. Can you imagine quoting Nikita Khrushchev saying "We will bury you?".
